About Us

Welcome to Euclid Academy Nurturing Minds, Shaping Futures! Established in 2019 with unwavering dedication and passion by our founder and teacher, Dipesh Pradhan. Euclid Academy began as a humble home tuition initiative. Through years of hard work and commitment, it has evolved into a reputable educational institution that strives for excellence in every aspect of a student's learning journey.

Our journey doesn't stop here; it's a continuous pursuit of academic and personal growth. We extend our heartfelt gratitude to our students and parents for believing in our institution. Your trust inspires us to innovate, adapt, and create an enriching environment for learning.

Engage in our quiz competitions to stimulate critical thinking, embrace the power of words through our quotes challenges, unleash your creativity in our drawing classes, and explore the fascinating world of Vedic mathematics. These activities are designed to enhance cognitive abilities, foster creativity, and instill a sense of confidence and accomplishment in our students.

Thank you for choosing Euclid Academy Where Education meets Excellence!
